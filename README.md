-A legal documents summarizer model trained on over 100 docx's and pdf's collected across different legislation websites

-Data is cleaned with cleaner.py, and the important information of the files is extracted with the help of the intelligent_extractor script.

-Model T5 was used cause of its already profound understanding of the Vietnamese language, which all of the documents used for training is written in.

-The deployment on HuggingFace is realized with the Summ-model-deployment repo.
